About TTQ.PRO KITCHEN

Welcome to TTQ.PRO KITCHEN, where traditional recipes meet modern culinary excellence. Our passion for authentic flavors and dedication to quality ingredients has made us a beloved destination for food enthusiasts.

Founded with the vision of bringing authentic cuisine to our community, we take pride in every dish we serve. Our chefs combine time-honored techniques with innovative approaches to create unforgettable dining experiences.

At TTQ.PRO KITCHEN, we believe that great food brings people together. Whether you're dining in with family or taking away for a cozy night at home, we ensure every meal is prepared with care and attention to detail.

We source our ingredients from trusted local suppliers, supporting our community while guaranteeing freshness in every bite. Our menu features a variety of traditional dishes, each with its own story and unique flavor profile.

Privacy Policy

At TTQ.PRO KITCHEN, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide when contacting us through our website forms or placing orders. We also collect non-personal information such as browser type, IP address, and pages visited for analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information: The information we collect is used to respond to your inquiries, process your orders, improve our services, and send occasional updates about our specials and events (only if you've opted in). We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. All form submissions and payment information are transmitted securely.

Cookies: Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and track website analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain website functionalities.

Third-Party Services: We use trusted third-party services for payment processing and delivery, which may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their terms if you have concerns about data handling.

Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact form.

Changes to This Policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
